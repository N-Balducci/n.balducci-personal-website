My life as a volunteer started after a summer work period at the "Cooperativa sociale La Scatola Magica". This is a cooperative that provides play and recreational services for children aged between 4 and 11 years.
In addition to the range of activities it carries out with regard to the organisation of events, parties, sporting activities, theatre, and summer centres, and of course, being a 'small' cooperative, it also requires the possibility of being able to do voluntary work within it as an entertainer for the most part.
I immediately jumped at the chance because I am a person who has always been used to being in contact with other children since I was a child and the idea of being able to help in some way with my contribution makes me particularly happy and excited!

In this section I want to briefly present a project to which I am particularly attached and that was born really recently.
The activity in question concerns the opening of a multi-sports association that takes the name of Magica Pyxis, thanks to which, through my initiative and the close collaboration with the "Cooperativa sociale la Scatola Magica", it is possible to take advantage of many services in terms of sports activities, from the best known to the less practiced.
My idea comes from the need to want to restart and especially to restart the children and young people who during the period of the pandemic have been severely affected by the dark periods of lockwodn and various closures.
The collaboration with a beautiful cooperative as the Magic Box, has allowed me to embark on this path.
So here is the last piece for the moment of my activities today, a piece that results in the figure of "President of the Polisportiva Association" and that wants to welcome all those who visit this web page!
